# Configuration and Environment

## Campaign

- `AICC_TOPIC`
- `AICC_NICHE_PACK`
- `AICC_VARIANTS`

## Voice and Avatar

- `AICC_TTS_PROVIDER`
- `AICC_TTS_VOICE`
- `AICC_AVATAR_PROVIDER`
- `AICC_AVATAR_PRESET`

## Auto-Publish Adapters

YouTube:
- `YOUTUBE_PUBLISH_WEBHOOK`
- or `YOUTUBE_CLIENT_ID`, `YOUTUBE_CLIENT_SECRET`, `YOUTUBE_REFRESH_TOKEN`

Instagram:
- `INSTAGRAM_PUBLISH_WEBHOOK`
- or `IG_USER_ID`, `IG_ACCESS_TOKEN`

TikTok:
- `TIKTOK_PUBLISH_WEBHOOK`
- or `TIKTOK_ACCESS_TOKEN`

## Scheduling

- `AICC_SCHEDULE_SPACING_MIN`
- `AICC_VIDEO_ASSET`
